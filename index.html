What is DSC-NUS?

DSC NUS is made up of people from diverse backgrounds, majors, years of study, genders and races.

We come together to push our mission of #TechforGood.

What is the Technology team?

The Technology team is responsible for the execution of DSC-NUS workshops and thematic tech events. We also take up tech advisory roles for club-wide DSC-NUS events such as the upcoming Hack For Good hackathon.
